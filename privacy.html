Privacy Policy

How we handle your data

Effective date: January 1, 2025

Technous LTD ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting personal data we process in connection with our services, website, and communications.

Information we collect

  • Contact details such as name, email address, company, and role when you reach out via our forms or direct email.
  • Usage data including pages visited, browser type, and device information collected through analytics tools.
  • Project information voluntarily shared to scope engagements.

How we use information

  • Responding to inquiries and providing proposals.
  • Improving our website, services, and client experience.
  • Meeting legal obligations and maintaining security.

How we share information

We do not sell personal data. We share limited data with vetted service providers (such as hosting, analytics, and communication tools) under contractual obligations to protect confidentiality and security.

International transfers

Where data crosses borders, we implement appropriate safeguards consistent with applicable data protection laws.

Data retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy or comply with legal requirements.

Your rights

Subject to local laws, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your personal data. Contact us at hello@technous.dev to exercise these rights.

Security

We use administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect personal data, including encrypted transport, access controls, and periodic reviews of our security posture.

Children’s data

Our services are not directed to children under 16, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from them.

Updates

We may update this policy to reflect changes in practices or legal requirements. Material updates will be highlighted on this page.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to hello@technous.dev.
